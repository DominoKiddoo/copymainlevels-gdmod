# Copy Main Levels

Adds a button to easily copy the main levels of the game.

To copy a main level, click on <cy>the info button</cy> then the copy button <cr>in the popup</cr>. To copy the spin-off levels you will need the  [MoreGames](https://geode-sdk.org/mods/bitz.moregames) mod

#### Just a quick note
Some level names are **shortened** when copied as they do not fit into the character limit
